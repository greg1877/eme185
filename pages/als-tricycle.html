ALS Tricycle

Disability Reports, Davis, CA, USA

ID: alstrike

Relevant Skills: mechanical design, electrical design, software, ergonomics, biomechanics

The goal is to design and develop an electric tricycle that can be driven by someone with ALS or quadriplegia. Note that this is an early start senior design project and the team membesr have already been chosen. Please do not vote for this unless you are already on the team.
