Design of the bike shuttle bike storage compartment

BikeLeap, San Francisco, CA, USA

ID: bikeleap

Relevant Skills: mechanical design

A novel solution to long distance commutes is a combined bike+express bus system. By providing folding bike storage onboard a tour bus, commuters can ride to the express bus stop, load their bike, complete the intercity journey, and disembark o complete their journey by bike. But given the dimensions of a standard motor coach cargo hold, how many folded bikes can be efficiently loaded and by what mechanism? This project will require students to measure the UCD Med Center shuttle cargo hold and design a solution that can efficiently stow the maximum number of folding bikes.
